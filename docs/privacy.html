Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

1. Overview

Markleaf is a local-first Markdown note-taking app for Android. This privacy policy explains how Markleaf handles your data.

2. Data Collection

Markleaf does not collect any personal information. All your notes are stored locally on your device only.

We do NOT collect:

  • Name or email address
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Account or login information
  • Note content or tags
  • Usage statistics
  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Analytics or crash reports

3. Network Access

Markleaf does not use the internet permission. The app works completely offline.

<manifest xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android"
    package="com.markleaf.notes">
    <!-- No INTERNET permission -->
</manifest>

4. Data Storage

All your notes are stored in a local database on your device. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export or share it.

5. Data Export

You can export your data at any time:

  • Export individual notes as Markdown files
  • Export all notes as a ZIP backup
  • Share notes via Android share sheet
  • Copy notes to clipboard

6. Third-Party Services

Markleaf does not connect to any third-party services, servers, or APIs. There are no:

  • No cloud synchronization
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising
  • No user accounts required

7. Children's Privacy

Markleaf does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not have any age-restricted features.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page. For significant changes, we will announce via the app or our GitHub page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via GitHub.
